You and I You only love the brighter side but I cherish both. You see stain and do ignore but I embrace both. You seek for perfection but I accept both. You hate the wrong one but I never loathe. You search the ground hoping to find the gold. I dug it and found it now trying to mold. You judge it by the cover but I wait and behold. Till the end; You lose the hope but I remain bold.

Glimpse at it... Hanged, Ranged & Estranged in the walls for years & years. Hardly ever been gazed and stared at But, When I did, That frame worthed much more, than that I could ever have dreamnt One thing fascinating about it !! You stare, talk, quarrel or even fight, what you get in return is just 'Quiet' Up there for forever, being mere need to be taken cared as it seems to be very rare ...

Eyes of the Eternal Symphony of love, being paraded in your eyes, Deluding in such a way, being enmeshed with ties; Royal vague of obsession, leading the cognitive desonance, Shower some mercy on me, reviving my eternal resonance.

Leap towards a new Bloom Like a breeze entering the room, a sober soul of a child is consumed by gloom. Obligations in every step, hindering leaps to a new bloom. Still seeking a new path in this realm of doom. & With discrete ways to groom, a life is crafted leaping towards a new bloom...

Newness Stepping into the dawn of a new chapter, where endless possibilities awaits. Embracing the change, as life's tapestry always sways. Life's courses have changed, yet new paths awaits to be braved. Dreams have changed, yet possibilities still linger to be arranged. Perspectives have changed, unveiling a world entirely rearranged. Now, I am truly engaged, as my worldview has exchanged. New perspectives emerged, altering landscape of my aspirations. Fresh insights blossomed, shaping.
